💡 Exam Tips for Beginners

  • Questions often describe a scenario — read carefully before choosing an answer.
  • Performance-Based Questions (PBQs) appear first; don't spend too long on them.
  • When stuck, eliminate obviously wrong answers first — then pick the "most correct" option.
  • "Least privilege" and "defense in depth" are guiding principles behind many right answers.
  • Know the difference between authentication (who are you?) and authorization (what can you do?).
  • Study acronyms: CIA Triad, AAA, MFA, PKI, IDS/IPS, SIEM, SOC, DLP, MDM.
